Headquartered out of Chicago, IL, Dirt Robotics specializes in providing teleoperated heavy equipment robotics with advanced autonomous capabilites across the Midwest.

Incorporated in 2025, our engineering team has extensive previous experience in the developement of teleoperated and autonomous mobile robotic platforms.

Our skilled heavy equipment operators are highly experienced and certified for all the vehicles in our fleet of roboticized equipment. This team operates the machines day-to-day, and provides crucial input on the path towards true autonomy.
