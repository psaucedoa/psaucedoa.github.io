Safety

Build Without Worry

Eliminating Operator Exposure to Worksite Hazards

When all PPE is worn, all guidance adhered to, all tasks executed perfectly, and everything goes as expected, the worksite is an exceptionally safe environment. However, accidents can always happen.

Construction related occupations make up the third most dangerous occupations in the United States. Over the past decade, TBD people have lost life or limb in worksite-related accidents. This has led to countless lawsuits, settlements, and regulations, not to mention the human impact on families of those injured or killed.

It is our belief that the safest place for operators is not inside the cab, but behind a desk, far from the potential hazards of the worksite.
